Overview

We noticed the increasing trend in Cape Town for people wanting to make their spaces more 'green', so we decided it was time for someone to jump on the idea and to start offering a service which was easy user-friendly.

Together with my other partners we developed and created the brand, imagery, logistics planning, deliveries as well as all touch points with our customers.

The company had great response amongst our target audience but we were unlucky that in Cape Town at the time there was a huge drought and we felt that it would be wrong to continue the business in such severe conditions.
